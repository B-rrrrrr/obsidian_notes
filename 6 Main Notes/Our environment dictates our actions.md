# Our environment dictates our actions
Wir wollen uns in Stein und Pflanze übersetzt haben, wir wollen *in uns* spazieren gehen, wenn wir in diesen Hallen und Gärten wandeln.

Where we live, work and think influences the way we become what we are. Nowadays, we lack space for silence, for contemplation, with churches basically being the only kind of place for contemplation. Except the church bases itself on old ideologies and values, encoding all thought religiously. Instead, we need to create our own areas for ourselves, create new spaces for doing the things we need to do. We need to create ourselves into our environment.
